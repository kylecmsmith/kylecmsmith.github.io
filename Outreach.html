Outreach

Speaking in classrooms

Through the Skype a Scientist program, I have spoken in various K-12 classrooms about dogs, wolves, primates, and animal behavior in general. I am available to speak to classrooms around the world in English or Spanish. If you would like me to speak in your classroom, please contact me.

I can also speak in person at classrooms in the State College, PA, area. This can include giving live, interactive demonstrations of dog cognition where students can participate in the experiments themselves. Please contact me if interested.

Stories Under the Sky

Along with Abby Gancz, I co-run an event series called Stories Under the Sky. We tell myths and folktales about the stars, Sun, Moon, and sky from cultures all around the world. We also talk about how people have used the sky for navigation, timekeeping, and understanding the cosmos. Following the stories, we give a guided tour of the sky, including pointing out constellations and letting attendees look through a telescope. Past events have taken place at the Penn State Arboretum (funded by the Tygel Endowment) and AstroFest. Please contact me if interested in learning more or collaborating.
