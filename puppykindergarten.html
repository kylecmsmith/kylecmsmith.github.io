Duke Puppy Kindergarten Project

As the lab coordinator of the Duke Canine Cognition Center, I helped create and oversee the Duke Puppy Kindergarten program. Duke University undergraduates volunteer to help raise and train puppies. These puppies are part of Canine Companions and go on to be trained to help people with disabilities. I trained and supervised over 120 volunteers as they raised the program's first five puppies: Ernie, Kyler, Aiden, Ashton, and Dune.
